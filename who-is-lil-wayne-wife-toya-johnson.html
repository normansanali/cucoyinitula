Who is Lil Wayne wife Toya Johnson?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, August 11, 2024

Fans are more interested in other facets of Lil Wayne’s personal life than his political views, which are frequently in the news along with his music. For starters, Lil Wayne has several “baby mamas” and several kids of various ages.

The problem is that, at least thus far, he has only ever been married once. Dwayne Michael Carter Jr. and Toya Johnson, his high school sweetheart, welcomed their first child in 1998. They got married when Wayne was about 22 and only 16 years old.

Who is Lil Wayne’s wife Toya Johnson?

Perhaps Toya Johnson was Lil Wayne’s high school sweetheart when they first met. However, more than ten years after getting married to and divorcing the rapper, she is now pursuing her interests.

Toya has come a long way since the conception of her first child, a girl named Reginae with Dawyne Carter Jr. when she was just 14 years old. Johnson gave birth to her first child at the age of 15, and her second daughter would follow in 2018, exactly 20 years later.

Johnson and her current boyfriend (and reported fiancé), Red Rushing, have a second child together, Reign. However, Johnson was previously married to another rather well-known person before she started dating Red.
