Alix Bailey is an American artist who is famous for her creative portraits. She became more famous as the wife of Louis C.K.

Bailey was born on February 3, 1967, in Washington, D.C. She probably inherited the skill from her parents, William Bailey and Sandra Stone, who were both painters.

She grew up in New Haven, Connecticut, with her brother Ford.

The family often visited Italy.

Alix Bailey’s Education

The details of her education from childhood to high school are not clear.

Bailey has a lot of knowledge in the arts, and she decided to dedicate her whole life to painting from the start.

She attended Bennington College, a liberal arts college in Vermont, where she learned painting.

After that, Bailey continued her education with a Master’s in Fine Arts at Indiana University.

She was taught by famous painters and artists such as Sidney Tillim and Pat Adams at the university.

Alix is of the same age as Louis

Both Alix Bailey and Louis C.K. were born in 1967, making them the same age.

They first met when they were 18 years old.

How she met and married Louis C.K

During a New Year’s Eve gathering in Boston, Alix and Louis first encountered each other.

At that event, Louis was heavily under the influence of alcohol and made a preposterous proposal of marriage to Alix within just five minutes of meeting her.

Despite his advances, Alix simply ridiculed him without taking any action.

Nonetheless, they had the opportunity to meet again after fifteen years with the help of a mutual acquaintance.

This time around, Louis didn’t hesitate to propose to Alix, and she accepted it.

They commenced their romantic journey and ultimately tied the knot in 1995.

Alix Bailey Marriage and Family

Louis C.K. and Alix Bailey first met when they were 18 years old in 1986 at a Boston New Year’s Eve celebration.

The story goes that an intoxicated Louis proposed to Alix just five minutes after meeting her, which amused her greatly.

However, nothing came of it at that time.

Fortunately, they reconnected almost ten years later through a mutual friend and began a long-term relationship, eventually getting married in 1995.

They had two daughters together, Kitty Szekely born in 2002 and Mary Louise Szekely born in 2005.

Sadly, their marriage broke down shortly after the birth of their second child, and they got divorced in 2008.

They attended marriage counseling, but they couldn’t make each other happy.

Despite their divorce, they maintained an amicable relationship and share custody of their daughters.

Interestingly, in 2016, eight years after their divorce, they purchased a home in Greenwich, New York for over $500,000 where Alix currently resides with their children.

Alix Bailey Career

Alix Bailey pursued her lifelong passion by becoming a professional painter.

She honed her skills by creating portraits of herself and her loved ones.

Her art has gained recognition, and some of her pieces have been exhibited in well-known art shows and galleries across New York, Connecticut, and Italy.

As a result, she has been considered one of America’s most talented painters.

Alix Bailey is an active member of “The Painting Center,” a non-profit art gallery located in New York.

Besides her talent in painting, Alix Bailey also possesses a gift in acting, which is a common trait among many artists.

She has acted in several films, including “Searching for Nixon,” “Pootie Tang,” and “Spooky Steakout.”

In 1998, she made her acting debut in the comedy “Tomorrow Night,” which was written and produced by her then-husband, Louis C.K.

Alix Bailey Net Worth

Alix Bailey has made a name for herself as a painter and has earned a substantial income from her artistic endeavors.

Her net worth is estimated to be over $1 million, in addition to any wealth associated with her ex-husband.
