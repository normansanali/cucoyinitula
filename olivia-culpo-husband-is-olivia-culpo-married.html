Olivia Culpo Husband: Is Olivia Culpo Married?: Olivia Culpo, officially known as Olivia Frances Culpo was born on May 8, 1992, in Cranston, Rhode Island, United States to Susan Curran and Peter Culpo

She’s an American model, fashion influencer, social media personality, and actress who developed a love for entertainment at an early age, and in 2nd grade, she started learning how to play the cello.

She has played cello in the Rhode Island Philharmonic Youth Orchestra, the Rhode Island Philharmonic Chamber Ensemble, the Bay View Orchestra, and the Rhode Island All-State Orchestra.

She attended St. Mary Academy Bay View and later went to Boston University, but did not graduate. Olivia again attended the Brevard Music Center, in Brevard, North Carolina for 2 summers where she performed with the Boston Accompanietta.

As a model, Olivia Culpo was featured in the Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Issue. She won the Miss Rhode Island USA competition and went on to win Miss USA, as well as Miss Universe in 2012.

In November 2013, she crowned Gabriela Isler of Venezuela as her successor making her (Olivia) the 3rd Miss Universe from the USA to pass the title off to Venezuela, after Shawn Weatherly in 1981 and Chelsi Smith in 1996.

As an actress, Olivia has made starred in the reality television show, Model Squad and has played roles in several films including; The Other Woman, I Feel Pretty and Reprisal. She was also cast as the female lead in Venus as a Boy.

As an influencer, Olivia Culpo has partnered and collaborated with several beauty and fashion brands like L’Oréal, Kipling, and Uberliss. Although young, she has risen through the ranks to become one of the most sought-after acts in the entertainment space.
