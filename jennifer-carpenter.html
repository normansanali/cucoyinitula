Jennifer Carpenter is an American actor, voice actor, and producer who is known for her television roles like Debra Morgan in the Showtime crime drama series Dexter, Rebecca Harris in the CBS comedy-drama series Limitless, and Erica Shepherd in the NBC drama series The Enemy Within. Moreover, her film credits include The Exorcism of Emily Rose, Avengers Confidential: Black Widow & Punisher, Battle in Seattle, Gone, Seeking Justice, A Mouthful of Air, Brawl in Cell Block 99, The Devil’s Hand, Batman: Gotham by Gaslight, Dragged Across Concrete, Mortal Kombat Legends: Scorpion’s Revenge, and Mortal Kombat Legends: Battle of the Realms.
