Willie Cauley Stein Biography

Willie Cauley-Stein is an American professional basketball player who was born on August 18, 1993, in Spearville, Kansas, the United States as Willie Trill Cauley-Stein.

He plays for the Dallas Mavericks of the National Basketball Association (NBA), and Willie played college basketball with the Kentucky Wildcats. Willie previously played with the Sacramento Kings for four seasons before signing with the Golden State Warriors in the year 2019 offseason. In January 2020 Willie was traded to the Mavericks.
