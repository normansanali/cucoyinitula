Sevyn Streeter Net Worth - NewsNow Nigeria

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, September 21, 2024

Sevyn Streeter, with a net worth of $4 million, is a well-known singer and songwriter in the music industry. Born as Amber Denise Streeter on July 7th, 1986, in Haines City, Florida, she gained popularity as a member of girl bands such as Rich Girl and TG4. Over the years, she has contributed to her net worth by creating songs for various renowned artists including Tamar Braxton, Brandy, Fantasia, Alicia Keys, Ariana Grande, Usher, and Kelly Rowland. Sevyn embarked on her journey in the entertainment industry since 2001.

Sevyn’s first music contract was signed at the age of 15, and she was part of the girl band TG4 along with Keisha Henry, Ashley Gallo, and Davida Williams. However, the group did not achieve much success and eventually disbanded. She later joined the band Rich Girl under the supervision of Rich Harrison, but this project also faced setbacks leading to Sevyn’s return as a solo artist.

Despite the initial failures, Sevyn’s career took a positive turn through songwriting, where she contributed to hit songs for artists like 50 Cent, Ariana Grande, and Chris Brown. In 2012, she released her first single “I Like It”, and in 2013, her collaboration with Chris Brown on the song “It Will not Stop” was a major success. She further released her EPs which received chart recognition and even earned a nomination for The Soul Train Music Award in 2014.

Sevyn also contributed to the soundtrack album “Furious 7: Original Motion Picture Soundtrack” and has plans to release her first studio album, “On the Verge”, which is expected to significantly boost her net worth.

In her personal life, Sevyn was in a long-term relationship with the rapper B.o.B but is currently believed to be single.
