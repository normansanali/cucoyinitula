Meet Alicia Machado, the Ex-Girlfriend of Bobby Abreu

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, August 16, 2024

Bobby Abreu is a retired Venezuelan profession baseball outfielder who played eighteen (18) seasons in the Major League Baseball (MLB). During his hay days, he played for the Houston Astros, Philadelphia Phillies, New York Yankees, Los Angeles Angels of Anaheim, Los Angeles Dodgers, and New York Mets.

Abreu’s career in the Major League Baseball has been nothing short of exception, having been one of the only seven players in MLB history to ever record at least 900 career extra-base hits and steal at least 400 bases along with Barry Bonds, Ty Cobb, Tris Speaker, Craig Biggio, Honus Wagner, and Paul Molitor.

He is two-time All-Star, and has won a Gold Glove Award and a Silver Slugger Award once each. He has been a single-season league leader in games played (twice), doubles, and triples. He had two seasons in which he collected thirty home runs and thirty stolen bases, making him one of thirteen players to have achieved the 30–30 club twice in a career.

Who is Alicia Machado?

Yoseph Alicia Machado Fajardo, known simply as Alicia Machado was born on December 6, 1976 in Maracay, Aragua, Venezuela. She is the daughter of Spanish father and a Cuban mother, both of whom emigrated into the United States.

She began performing at a tender age and by age 4, she was already dancing. She took to acting when she turned 12. After high school, she enrolled at college but spent a year and half but left to pursue a career in modeling and soon started making appearances in commercials.

Machado contested for the 1995 Miss Maracay pageant and was adjudged the winner of the contest. She also represented the Yaracuy state in the 1995 Miss Venezuela pageant which she also won. In 1996, Machado became the fourth Venezuelan to be crowned Miss Universe.
