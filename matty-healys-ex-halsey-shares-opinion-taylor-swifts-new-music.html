Matty Healys Ex Halsey Shares Her Real Opinion On Taylor Swifts New Music In Telling Image

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, September 27, 2024

Highlights

  • Halsey supports Taylor Swift's new album, despite its supposed scathing songs about Matty Healy.
  • Matty Healy doesn't seem bothered by Taylor Swift's new music, stating he hasn't listened much.
  • Joe Alwyn, subject of Taylor's new album post-breakup insights, remains publicly silent on the matter.

Taylor Swift may have some scathing songs about Matty Healy on her new album The Tortured Poets Society, but his ex Halsey says she’s still a fan of Swift’s work.

Halsey shared her excitement for the new album by posting a photo of her boyfriend Ava Jogia wearing sweatpants with the TTPD logo. Though she didn’t write a caption, it’s clear Halsey is enjoying the music, despite Swift’s history with Healy.

Matty and Taylor dated briefly last year, but it didn’t sit well with many of her fans, given The 1975’s singers controversial reputation, which more recently included a series of degrading comments about Ice Spice.

How Halsey And Matty Healy Responded To Taylor’s Album

The relationship wasn’t doing well for Taylor’s brand, and it came to an end as quickly as it started. It’s believed several of hew new songs, including “But Daddy I Love Him,” are about the controversial singer, though Taylor hasn’t confirmed any of the tracks on her recent record are about.

Halsey and Matty dated much earlier than that, for a moment in 2015. They’re both known for referring to each other in post-break-up music, including Halsey’s song “Colors.”

Matty seemed to respond to Taylor’s new work by saying he hasn’t given it much thought. “I haven’t really listened to that much of it,” he told TMZ. “I’m sure it’s good.”

Why Joe Alwyn Won’t Publicly Acknowledge Taylor’s New Music

Taylor is believed to reference many more famous folks in her new record, including Kim Kardashian, who appears to have gotten several diss tracks (and lost hundreds of thousands of IG followers, too). Of course, it’s suspected her current boyfriend Travis Kelce also gets a few shout-outs. His mom Donna Kelce recently said she plans to ask Taylor which songs are about her son.

But perhaps no one gets as much attention on the album as Joe Alwyn. Joe and Taylor announced their break-up in April 2023, after six years of dating, and Swift appears to have given various insights into their shocking split in the album. Some fans are convinced he cheated on her in Florida, while others believe they were engaged, or he at least was struggling to commit to marriage.

Despite the attention surrounding Joe right now, reports say the British actor has no plans to publicly acknowledge the album. It’s also been reported that the notoriously private celebrity is happily dating .

“Joe has not said one word about Taylor or their breakup and has been completely respectful of her,” a source told Daily Mail before the album’s release. “It’s undeniable that the name of her upcoming album is in reference to Joe’s WhatsApp group chat.”

“If it is a diss album, that is shady. He helped her with songwriting on her past album [under the pseudonym William Bowery on Folklore and Evermore], so it will really come as a shock to him if she talks about their breakup, as it is something he has not spoken of at all,” they continued.

“Regardless of what she does, he will still not respond because he has removed himself from her narrative and is very glad he did,” the source concluded.
