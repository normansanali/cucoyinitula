Who do you fight before Stormveil Castle?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, August 30, 2024

Before Stormveil Castle in Elden Ring, players have to defeat the boss named Margit, The Fell Omen. Margit guards the entrance to Stormveil Castle and players won’t be able to progress into the castle until they defeat him. Margit is a powerful boss in the early game, with a long reach and relentless attack combos.
