Pokimane is a famous Moroccan-Canadian video game streamer and an Instagram personality. Being a Twitch streamer and YouTuber, Pokimane is popular for her live streams on Twitch, presenting video game content, especially in League of Legends and Fortnite.

Early Life

The Instagram star was born on 14th May 1996, in Morocco, Borth Africa. Pokimane’s real name is Imane Any and she holds Moroccan and Canadian nationality. Also, she is from a mixed ethnicity and follows Christianity. Similarly, her age is twenty-seven years and her sun sign is Taurus. Regarding her education, she went to McMaster University. Career and Professional Life

Twitch Streaming career

After she got out of school, she achieved 450 thousand followers on Twitch in the year 2017, placing her account a place within the hundred most followed on the platform. In the year 2017, her account’s extended, and as a result, the Shorty Awards gave her a name as the Best Twitch Streamer of the year. The Shorty Awards also said that her gameplay and commentary streams of League of Legends helped her to achieve more fame on Twitch. Any had a cameo presence in a League of Legends trailer declaring another game mode.

She has been popular for streaming gameplay and explanation of Fortnite, which she 1st streamed as a member of a sponsorship. At E3 in the year 2018, Epic Games, the developers of Fortnite, managed a pro-am occasion. The event matched streamers with mainstream stars in a match of Fortnite’s Battle Royale mode. Any was matched with rapper Desiigner but soon before the event, his place was taken by the basketball player Josh Hart. In mid-March 2019, she directed her reducing amount of Fortnite streams, declaring that she needed to “think about what I like or dislike about the content that I’ve been making”.
