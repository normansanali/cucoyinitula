Best Weekend Getaway Electric Cooler

The compact yet powerful Dometic CFX3 ($1,194) is perfect for a weekend car and tent camping adventure. It’s big enough (35 liters) to handle beverages and a few meals, but small enough to easily carry a short distance into a campsite.

It features a single zone with a convenient control panel display, and comes with Dometic’s quality reputation and product support.

Best Affordable Heavy-Duty Electric Cooler (Also Best for Tailgating)

The rugged ICECO VL45 Portable Fridge ($609) can handle the bumps and vibrations of off-road conditions. Featuring a metal shell and thickened inner wall, it can be operated at up to 40% tilt. It’s a single-zone cooler with one lid that can be set to various temperatures.

Other features of this portable fridge include metal latches to keep it from accidentally popping open, along with max cooling and energy-efficiency settings. It fits the bed height of a pickup truck, so it’s good for tailgating as well. There are some fancier off-road coolers around, but for three times the price.

Best Job Site and Best Beach Day Electric Cooler

You can’t beat the convenience of Worx’s 20-volt Electric and Battery Powered Cooler ($499), which runs off the same Worx batteries that power more than 75 tools in the Power Share family. Unlike most other models, it comes with two 20-volt batteries, which you can charge with your solar panels.

It can cool to 32 degrees in just 15 minutes, and ultimately cool to -4 degrees. Its lighter weight, roller wheels and heavy-duty pull handle also make it ideal to zip down the boardwalk or into the park. It comes with topside cup holders and a bottle opener for picnicking, along with a USB charging port.
