Ansel Elgort Attends With Girlfriend Violetta Following Assault Allegations

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, August 2, 2024

In 2020, Baby Driver star Ansel Elgort was at the center of major public backlash, following allegations that he sexually assaulted a 17-year-old woman in New York City in 2014. The user identified herself as "Gabby," in a post that detailed the assault, and has since been deleted.

After the allegations made against him, Elgort took to social media to express his "distress" over the accusation, denying that he assaulted Gabby. The actor also said that he dated her in 2014, describing their relationship as a "brief, legal and entirely consensual" one.

Ansel Elgort Attends New Movie Premiere

Elgort attended a premiere of his upcoming Steven Spielberg-directed film West Side Story, alongside longtime ballerina girlfriend Violetta Komyshan. The pair were reported to have broken up after not being spotted together for months, but they made a rare public appearance on November 29.

Komyshan looked stunning as usual in a gold, strapless gown, while the 27-year-old actor donned a black tuxedo.

Some Twitter users are horrified at Elgort's comeback to the movies, especially after he allegedly sought out underage girls and sent them unsolicited photos.
