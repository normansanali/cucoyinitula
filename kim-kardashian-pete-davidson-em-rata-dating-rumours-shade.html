Kim Kardashian Fans Say She Shaded Pete Davidson Over Emily Ratajkowski Rumours

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, September 5, 2024

Someone get Pete Davidson and Emily Ratajkowski some ice for their burns, because fans think Kim Kardashian might've just thrown shade at their relationship.

As The Kardashians viewers will know, Kim and Pete started dating after meeting on Saturday Night Live in October 2021. While they kept things under wraps for a while, the pair eventually went Instagram—and even red carpet—official, before calling it quits in spring.

More recently, dating rumors between Pete and model Emily Ratajkowski emerged, with a source telling E! News they had "gone on a few dates" last week. The insider added that "Pete texted Emily wanting to get together to catch up", with Instagram gossip account Deux Moi saying they were spotted "holding hands" and "clearly hooking up."

Now, fans think Kim Kardashian might've just referenced her ex-boyfriend's new relationship on TikTok. Kimmy shared a video dancing and mouthing along to Ariana Grande's 'Santa Tell Me', and it didn't take long for people to clock that Ariana is none other than Pete's ex-fiancée. Drama, chaos, etc etc.

"The same day news comes out about Pete and Emily. Huh?" one fan commented, while another said, "Kim really fired up a TikTok to Ariana Grande after the Pete & EmRata news."

Someone else added, "Kim K using Ariana Grande music in a TikTok oh it’s happening."

Ariana and Pete got together in May 2018, before the SNL comedian proposed months later. Sadly it wasn't to be—by the following October, they had called things off, with Ariana later tying the knot to husband Dalton Gomez in 2021.

Of course, this is all just speculation—Kim and North could easily just be getting in the Christmas spirit... Right?
