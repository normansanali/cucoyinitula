Dane DeHaan Height: How tall is Dane DeHaan?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, September 24, 2024

American actor Dane DeHaan stands at 5 ft 8 inches (1.73 m & 173 cm) tall.

DeHaan also weighs 75 kg (165lbs).

Who Is Dane DeHaan?

Dane DeHaan an American actor known for appearing as Harry Osborn / Green Goblin in the 2014 superhero film The Amazing Spider-Man 2.

Born on February 6, 1986, in Allentown, Pennsylvania, United States, DeHaan began acting in 2008 in Broadway productions, and later on an episode of Law & Order: Special Victims Unit.

In 2010, DeHaan starred as Jesse in the third season of HBO’s In Treatment. Since then he has appeared in many feature films and TV series including True Blood, Chronicle, Lawless, The Place Beyond the Pines, Kill Your Darlings, and The Amazing Spider-Man 2.

Other films in which he also starred are A Cure for Wellness, Valerian and the City of a Thousand Planets, ZeroZeroZero, Lisey’s Story, Metallica: Through the Never, The Stranger, Two Lovers and a Bear, The Staircase, and the 17th-century romance Tulip Fever.

DeHaan has once appeared on the cover of the magazine “Another Man”, and “Hero magazine”, and has been a model for Prada’s men’s clothing.

For his role in film, DeHaan has received a few accolades, including a BAFTA nomination.
