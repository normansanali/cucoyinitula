Are Lauren Williams And Porsha Williams Real Sisters?

No, they aren’t real sisters if real means sharing the same parents. Lauren Williams and Porsha Williams are half-sisters who share the same father. The sisters share a close bond and it strengthened even more after they become mothers to their daughters. In May 2021, Lauren took to her Instagram the snap of her time with her daughter Baleigh and her niece Pilar Jhena. She wrote, “They say having a niece is like having a 2nd daughter to love and it really is… so can I claim her on my taxes too? #askingforafriend”

They are among the four children Diane T. Williams and Hosea Williams II had. They have two brothers named Hosea Williams III and Brenton Williams.

On the 10 January 2021 episode, her sister opened up about dealing with her parent’s divorce. She told, “It was just kind of confusing at first because I had my home with Mommy that felt complete, solid. And then over here with Dad was another home that could be complete and solid, and why am I going in between?”

About growing up with Lauren, Porsha also shared, “She’s lived with Daddy, and I more so came over on the weekends but saying ‘half anything would get you a spanking in the house.”

Her father passed away in 1998 of complications from a bone marrow transplant, after being diagnosed with a rare form of leukemia the year prior. He died at the age of 43. At that time, Lauren was only 10 years old.

Lauren shared about her father, “I remember him waking up in the morning, kissing me on the forehead, and going to work, not coming back until it was time to go to bed,”

As for her mom, Diane is an entrepreneur and public speaker. She runs a daycare business. She also appeared on “The Real Housewives of Atlanta” in a total of 24 episodes of the show.
