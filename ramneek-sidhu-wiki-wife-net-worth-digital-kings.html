Meet Ramneek Sidhu, The Owner Of Digital Kings

Like most of the successful young entrepreneurs, Ramneek grew up looking up to people like Jeff Bezos, Bill Gates, and Mark Zuckerburg. Even in his lowest points, these person’s stories would motivate him and made him work towards his dream.

Ramneek’s entrepreneurial journey in June 2016 started with the formation of Digital Kings, a digital marketing and advertising company that works with Bollywood stars, as well as Hollywood celebrities and musicians.

Digital Kings aims to maintain and multiply the social media presence of their clients. They also offer branding, web designs, and movie promotions among other things. For growing businesses and brand-new startups, Digital Kings helps them “generate more profits by building awareness, driving web traffic, connecting with customers, and growing overall sales.”

In just a few years of Digital Kings‘ formation, the Indian native has expanded his business to Dubai and is now planning to open a branch in Canada as well.

Ramneek has always had one mantra for success. Ramneek thinks qualities like skills, patience, and determination are a must to become a successful entrepreneur. It is because of these qualities and his inspirational entrepreneurial journey, Ramneek today has become the idol for young minds in and out of India.

Currently, Ramneek also works at VS Records as its owner. He started working there on October 20, 2017. Previously, Ramneek used to work as Head of Digital at EYP Creations Pvt. Ltd in Sahibzada Ajit Singh Nagar, Punjab, India from January 2013 to October 2016.

In 10 years from now, Ramneek sees himself “working and designing novel thoughts like Jeff Bezos, Bill Gates, and Mark Zuckerberg and as a productive enthusiast.” He also said he wants to deal with new ideas in the future.

For his education, Ramneek went to Golden Bells Public School. He did his BTech in Computer Science from Rayat and Bahra Group of Institutes.

Ramneek Sidhu Net Worth

Ramneek Sidhu’s net worth was estimated to be around $200 thousand as of October 2021. But, the way Ramneek’s business Digital Kings is growing, his net worth could be in millions and billions in coming years.

Ramneek is close to Subi’s sister, Bhumika Yadav, as well. He wished her a happy birthday on November 17, 2020, as well.

From what it looks like from the outside, Ramneek and Subi have plenty of love and respect for each other. However, they are still unmarried and Subi is still not Ramneek’s wife.
