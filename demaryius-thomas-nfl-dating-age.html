Demaryius Thomas is an American football wide receiver currently playing for the New York Jets of the National Football league (NFL). He played college football at the Georgia Institute of Technology for three seasons.

Demaryius Thomas Bio; Parents, Education, Height, Weight

Demaryius Antwon Thomas was born on 25 December 1987 to parents Bobby Thomas and Katina Smith. He grew up with two sisters; Tonecia and Tyeshia Smith in Montrose, Georgia.

Thomas pursued his early education at West Laurens High School in Dexter, Georgia. Later, he went to the Georgia Institute of Technology, where he majored in management. He stands at a height of 6 feet 3 in (1.91 meters) and weighs 229 lbs (104 kg).

His Career; NFL, Stats

Demaryius played college football at Georgia Tech Yellow Jackets football for three seasons from 2007 to 2009. He finished his collegiate career with 120 catches for a conference-leading 2,339 yards and 14 touchdowns.
