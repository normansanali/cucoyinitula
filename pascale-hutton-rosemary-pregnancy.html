Pascale Huttons Rosemary: Pregnancy Speculations in When Calls the Heart

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, July 11, 2024

Hallmark’s drama series “When Calls the Heart,” which is based on Janette Oke’s book of the same name, has been a fan favourite ever since it debuted in 2014. It centres on Elizabeth Thatcher, a young teacher who moves to Hope Valley, a small coal mining settlement on the Western frontier. She discovers love, purpose, and several lovable companions, including Rosemary Coulter (Pascale Hutton), as she lets go of her high society existence and loves her new home.

After seeing Rosemary struggle to conceive for a time, fans have been left wondering whether she is indeed pregnant in light of the events of season 9. Additionally, they have been speculating as to whether actress Pascale Hutton is also expecting a kid in real life. Let’s investigate the matter further, shall we? Spoilers follow.

Is Rosemary Pregnant in When Calls the Heart Season 10?

Since season 6, Rosemary and Lee have excitedly sought to conceive a child, but each time they have only experienced heartbreak and pain. In particular, the former is upset by her struggle with infertility and longs for a small child to live in her own house despite being glad for her friends who have children, especially Elizabeth. When sisters Lillian and Grace visit Hope Valley with the orphaned children in their custody, the Coulters even consider adoption. Lee and Rosemary continue to hold out hope for a kid of their own even though nothing seems to go as planned for them.

Thankfully, in season 9 episode 10, when Rosemary becomes ill and thinks she could have stomach flu, the couple’s prayers are ultimately answered. However, she finds out that she might be pregnant when she sees Faith for a medical evaluation. Rosemary slowly tells her ecstatic husband the news, albeit she initially keeps it a secret until she can confirm it. Elizabeth, her best friend’s confidante, is informed later and is thrilled to hear the good news.

Time flies rapidly as Rosemary and Lee begin making preparations for their child, and in episode 1 of season 10, she is only a few weeks away from giving birth. Even though the pair is somewhat coping with the town’s financial crisis as a result of the foundry’s closure, they continue to assist as many Hope Valley families as they can. Elizabeth steps in to assist Rosemary in the meantime, even giving her some used pregnancy clothing to make life easier for her. The soon-to-be mother confides in her buddy about her anxieties and worries that she won’t make a decent parent. Elizabeth, though, reassures Rosemary that she will be the best mother to her unborn child and consoles her.

Is Pascale Hutton Pregnant?

Pascale Hutton is not pregnant, much as fans would have loved to see the reel and reality collide. In truth, the actress has been using a prosthetic baby belly to film her pregnant sequences for season 10. In a July 2023 Instagram post, Pascale revealed a similar fact, writing, “This was my first day filming season 10 with Rosemary’s baby bump!! Beginning on July 30, I can’t wait to share the pregnant journey with you.

Although Pascale keeps her private life private off-screen, we do know that she married actor Danny Dorosh in 2002. The actress and her husband of more than fifteen years don’t share many details about their two sons on social media. Nevertheless, Pascale is happy with her gorgeous family and has not mentioned having more children. Fans’ speculation that she might be expecting like her character in “When Calls the Heart” just goes to show how convincing she is in that role.
