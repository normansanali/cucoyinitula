Wikipedia hasn’t highlighted the death case of Teresa Sievers thus far. The holistic medicine physician was brutally murdered six years ago.

Teresa Sievers is an American woman who was mercilessly murdered in Florida.

It was on the 28th of June when she was bludgeoned to death by two men.

Consequently, the news spread like a wildfire and made several headlines.

In the article let’s learn about her death and the reason behind the murder.

Teresa Sievers Wikipedia And Family

Teresa Sievers’s Wikipedia bio hasn’t been highlighted thus far on the web.

In any case, several news portals have covered insights in regards to her death.

Sievers was reported to be 46 years old at the occurrence of the incident.

The 46-year-old ran a holistic medical practice named Restorative Health and Healing Center.

On the weekend of July 2015, Teresa was bludgeoned to death by two men.

Teresa, her husband, and two daughters had travelled to New York before she returned home alone to see patients.

Reportedly, the two men were disclosed to be Jimmy Ray Rodgers and Curtis Wayne Wright.

In fact, it was discovered that her husband was the friend of Curtis Wright since high school.

Teresa Sievers Husband: Mark Sievers

Teresa Sievers’s husband Mark Sievers is on death row at Floria State Prison in Raiford.

Previously he was on trial for the brutal murder of his wife, Tereasa Sievers.

Allegedly, the couple shared two daughters Josephine Sievers and Carmela Sievers.

Shreds of evidence were directly towards Mark not long after the investigation started.

He seemed to have fake grief stated by Lieutenant David Lebid at the time of interrogation.

Mark recruited the two men to kill Teresa according to Wright’s testimony.

The reason behind such act was disclosed to be marriage problems and fear of losing daughter’s custody.

In the response, Mark’s attorney defended Wright’s claim to be baseless allegations.

However, the prosecutors presented evidence of Mark and Wright’s communication.

Mark was convicted of first-degree murder and conspiracy to commit murder of Teresa.

Teresa Sievers Murder- How Did She Die?

Teresa Sievers was murder by two men named on June 28, 2015, in her Bonita Springs home.

She was murdered brutally by them with a hammer used in the attack.

Jimmy Ray Rodgers and Curtis Wayne Wright got into a rental car set to kill Teresa.

Reportedly, they were 17 hours away from Bonita Springs, Florida.

Insights Of Teresa Sievers Children Case update

Speaking of Teresa Sievers’s children case update, her mother Mary Ann Groves was granted custody.

Her mother, Mary applied for temporary custody of her granddaughters given their psychological state.

Allegedly, she was granted custody of Teresa’s daughter on the 16th of May 2016.

Mark’s mother Bonnie was denied upon requesting the transfer of the temporary custody to her.

The girls were placed with another family in the area prior to their temporary custody by the maternal grandmother.

As of now, Josephine is 17 years old and Carmela is 14 years old.
