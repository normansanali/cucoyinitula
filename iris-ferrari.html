Iris Ferrari is a Famous YouTuber. Iris Ferrari is famous for her self-titled YouTube channel with 701K subscribers. In her channel, she posts videos related to personal stories, eating videos, and challenges. She also has a heavy following on Instagram and TikTok.

Early Life and Childhood

Professional Life

Iris Ferrari gave a start to her career as a YouTuber. She opened her self-titled YouTube channel ‘Iris Ferrari‘ on June 10, 2014. On her channel, she posts content related to stories, beauty-related videos, and challenges.

In her first video, she is seen interviewing her mother, to check how well she knows her. Likewise in 2016, she uploaded a video series on Tiktok themes where she discussed how to create content for Tiktok. She also taught about how to make audio, how to use tags, and many other Tiktok-related topics.

Caption: Iris (Source: Youtube)

After her creative content on YouTube, she was recognized by many of her fans.  She was signed by the company, NewCo Management, for participating in ‘meet and greet’ events across the country in February 2017. The first event was held in Piazza Duomo in Milan and was attended by 2000 people.

Many of her videos have over a million views at present and some of those videos are ‘Le Mie Cover Per iPhone 6s’ ‘Reagiamo Ai Vecchi Musical.ly Di Luciano Spinelli,’ and ‘Musical.ly Compilation #1′ among many.

Likewise in February 2018, she published her autobiography titled ‘Una di voi’, which means ‘One of You’, published by Mondadori Electa. The book was so popular that 1000 copies were sold in just a week. In the book, Iris has narrated her life stories and has talked about her journey as a social media star. The book is an inspiration for all her fans who want to follow in her footsteps and it is listed as one of the highest-ranking books by teenagers in Italy.
