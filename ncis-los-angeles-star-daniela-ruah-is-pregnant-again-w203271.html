NCIS: Los Angeles Star Daniela Ruah Is Pregnant Again

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

No. 2! NCIS: Los Angeles' Daniela Ruah is pregnant and expecting her second child with husband David Olsen, she announced via Instagram on Monday, April 18.

The actress, 32, shared a sweet snapshot of herself — with the slightest baby bump — exercising outdoors with her hubby and their son, Isaac, 2. Ruah wrote alongside the photo: “There are 4 people in this photo, can you spot them all?”

The Portuguese-American TV star and excited soon-to-be-second-time mom affectionately added the following hashtags: “#AnotherRuahOlsenontheway #addingtothefamily #Autumnbaby #feelinggrateful #love #parenthood.”

Ruah and Olsen first met on the set of NCIS: Los Angeles. While Ruah plays Special Agent Kensi Blye, former Navy SEAL Olsen, 40, serves as the stunt double for his younger brother, Eric Christian Olsen, who portrays Detective Marty Deeks on the CBS drama.

Eric and his actress wife, Sarah Wright Olsen, will be welcoming their own bundle of joy later this year. In February, the couple shared the happy news by posting an adorable pic of their 2-year-old son, Wyatt, cradling his mother’s belly.
