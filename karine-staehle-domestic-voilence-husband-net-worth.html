The video was out in the internet through Foxtech Marketing’s YouTube channel where raw footage of the reality star couple in their home in Louisville, Kentucky. Who posted this? And if it’s Paul and Karine leaking this, why? By influence? Staying relevant? Or is it a call from help from Paul? I need answers and they need a divorce.”
