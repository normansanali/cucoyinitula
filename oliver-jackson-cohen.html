The Invisible Man's Actor, Oliver Jackson-Cohen Is Dating His Girlfriend, Jessica De Gouw Since 2013

Oliver Jackson-Cohen is a British actor and model who is best known for starring Jonathan Harker in Dracula from 2013 to 2014. Also, he is known for his roles in the Netflix TV programmes, as Luke Crain in The Haunting of Hill House (2018) and as Peter Quint in The Haunting of Bly Manor (2020).

In 2020, he has starred as Adrian Griffin in the American science fiction horror film, The Invisible Man. Let's find out more interesting details about him in the following sections. 

Who is Oliver Jackson-Cohen Dating?

34, Oliver Jackson-Cohen is no more single. As of now, he is in a relationship with his fellow star, Jessica De Gouw, 33. She is an Australian actress who is famous for her role Helena Bertinelli/The Huntress in the series Arrow from 2012 to 2014.

Further, the stars have rarely shown their love and affection on their social media account. As of now, Oliver and Jessica are set to tie the wedding knot very soon but there is no news about it yet.

Bio and Early Life

Oliver Jackson-Cohen was born on 24 October 1986 in Westminster, London, United Kingdom. His birth name is Oliver Mansour Jackson-Cohen. He is the son of David Cohen, a businessman and Betty Jackson, a fashion designer. 

Cohen has an older sister Pascale Jackson-Cohen. He attended the French-language school and later joined the Youngblood Theatre Company where he began working for a florist in washing vases. Furthermore, he studied at the Lee Strasberg Theatre and Film Institute in NYC.
