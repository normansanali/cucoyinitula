Get to know Darcey Silvas daughters Aniko and Aspen Bollok on IG

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, July 6, 2024
Spread the love

Darcey and Stacey Silva, stars of the hit show “90 Day Fiancé,” returned with their own spin-off series, “Darcey & Stacey,” offering a glimpse into their lives filled with love, family, and business ventures. Season 2 promises even more excitement for fans.

Aniko Bollok: The Bright Young Star

Aniko Bollok, one of Darcey Silva’s daughters, stands at the cusp of adulthood, likely around 16 or 17 years old as of 202In an endearing moment on “Darcey & Stacey,” Darcey imparts some motherly wisdom to Aniko and her sister before a Sweet Sixteen celebration.

A social media sensation, Aniko can be found on Instagram under the handle @aspen.bollok, where she boasts an impressive following of around 59k. Her TikTok account, too, draws attention with 13.4k dedicated followers.

Aniko: The Guiding Light

Aniko, the older of the Bollok sisters, has been a source of support for her mother during pivotal moments on “90 Day Fiancé.” Her wisdom and guidance were especially vital after Darcey’s separation from Tom Brooks in 2020.

Currently a student at The University of Arizona, Aniko is set to spread her wings and graduate in 2026, marking a significant milestone in her academic journey.

Her Instagram presence (@aniko.bollok) is nothing short of impressive, boasting a loyal following of 74.5k. Additionally, on TikTok, Aniko commands a dedicated audience of 86.2k followers.

Aniko and Aspen: Kindred Spirits

While not twins like their dynamic mother-daughters duo, Aniko and Aspen share a special bond likely forged by their close ages. Previously residing in Connecticut with Darcey, Aniko has since embarked on a new chapter in Arizona for her university studies.

Their father, Frank Bollok, appears to call New York home, as gleaned from his Facebook profile.

Conclusion: A Supportive Family Unit

Aniko and Aspen Bollok, the daughters of the beloved Darcey Silva, are emerging as remarkable individuals in their own right. Their supportive presence has been a cornerstone for their mother during both joyous and challenging moments. As they chart their individual courses, the Bollok sisters are leaving their mark on the world, embodying resilience, wisdom, and unity. The future holds boundless opportunities for these bright young stars.
