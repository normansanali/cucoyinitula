Kendall Jenner's Net Worth Is Impressive — But Not Compared to Her Sisters'

Kendall Jenner is one of the lowest-earning members of the Kardashian-Jenner family — but her net worth is still impressive!

By Sara Belcher

Jun. 3 2024, Published 7:08 p.m. ET

The Kardashian-Jenner family is known for their wealth, and even the least wealthy of the family is still wealthier than most people. Between their reality show, various brand deals, and independent businesses, each member of the famous family has multiple streams of revenue.

Kendall Jenner is the second-youngest Kar-Jenner, and while she's also found success thanks to Keeping Up With the Kardashians and The Kardashians, her modeling career is what she's arguably best known for.

But with a successful career walking the runway for brands like Calvin Klein, Chanel, and Michael Kors, Kendall is independently wealthy. So what is her current net worth?

What is Kendall Jenner's net worth?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Kendall is currently estimated to be worth around $60 million. This makes her one of the lowest-earning members of the family (alongside Khloe Kardashian) — but $60 million still makes her a solid part of the one percent.

In 2017, Kendall was named the world's highest-paid supermodel, usurping Gisele Bündchen, who had held the title for the previous 15 years.

It's estimated she's earned as much as $40 million in one year from modeling alone, allotting for a good two-thirds of her net worth. She's also been part of publicity campaigns for brands like Marc Jacobs, Karl Lagerfeld, Fendi, Estée Lauder, and Calvin Klein Jeans. In addition to this branding work, Kendall has also collaborated with a variety of brands to create some signature products.

In 2011, Kendall had two polish colors as part of the family's collaboration with Nicole by OPI. She and Kylie also had their own collections debut at PacSun and Topshop in the 2010s, before branding off and exploring their own individual brands.

But while pursuing her individual product collaborations, Kendall also took the time to collaborate with her sisters. Kendall had a series of cosmetics launch as part of Kylie Cosmetics, her sister Kylie Jenner's makeup brand.

In addition to other miscellaneous collaborations and product launches, Kendall launched her tequila brand 818 Tequila in 2021. It's her most recent business venture and has remained one of her primary promotions, in addition to her modeling and various magazine cover shoots.

Kendall is one of the lowest-earning members of the Kardashian-Jenner family.

Kendall's family is known for its wealth, and though $60 million is nothing to scoff at, she's significantly less wealthy than many of her sisters. Kim Kardashian is currently the highest-earning member of the family, as she's estimated to be worth $1.7 billion. Kendall's sister Kylie isn't far behind, as she's estimated to be worth $700 million. Kourtney is only slightly more wealthy than Kendall and Khloe, though their mother is solidly in the middle of the bunch, earning an estimated $170 million.
