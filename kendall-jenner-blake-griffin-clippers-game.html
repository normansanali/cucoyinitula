Kendall Jenner Supports Blake Griffin at a Clippers Game

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, August 28, 2024

Kendall Jenner supported her man Blake Griffin from the sidelines of his first basketball game of the season last night. The model was spotted courtside at the Staples Center, where she watched Griffin and the LA Clippers face off with (and defeat) the LA Lakers.

The 21-year-old cheered, smiled, and snacked on some popcorn from her seat, wearing a tan checkered blazer, white tee, ripped jeans, and white heeled mules for the event.

Getty Images

She also shared a shot of her view of the court—literally—on Snapchat.

Snapchat

Though she came to support her beau, Jenner watched the game with a friend (perhaps to make her everything-but-confirmed romance with Griffin a little less conspicuous).

Getty Images

Meanwhile, Griffin did his thing on the court.

Getty Images

Jenner, 21, and Griffin, 28, were first rumored to be linked at the end of August when they were spotted out together. In early September, word broke that the model was dating both the NBA star and rapper A$AP Rocky, whom she was reportedly seeing for about a year.

Now, however, it seems Kendall is only hanging out with Blake. Prior to last night's game, she and the basketball player have recently been seen on outings together, including a double date with her sister Kylie Jenner and her beau, Travis Scott.

We wouldn't be surprised if we saw Kendall frequenting the floor seats at Clippers games for the rest of the season.
