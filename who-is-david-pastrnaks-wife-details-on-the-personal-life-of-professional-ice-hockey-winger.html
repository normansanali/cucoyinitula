He has represented the Czech Republic’s national teams at every level. He has also led his team in scoring numerous times.

In 2016, David joined the Czech Republic national team for the first time at IIHF World Championship. Pastrnak also represented the Czech Republic at the 2016 World Cup of Hockey.
