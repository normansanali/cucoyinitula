Lisa Rinna: Actress, Television Personality, Age, Husband, Daughters, Net Worth !

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, July 8, 2024

Lisa D. Rinna is an American based actress and television personality. As an actress, she is popular for her role on the NBC daytime soap opera Days of Our Lives as Billie Reed and Taylor McBride on Fox’s television drama Melrose Place. For 8 seasons, Lisa was a main cast member on Bravo’s superhit reality television series titled The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills. Additionally, she has a few other television credits being a contestant on ABC’s Dancing with the Stars and NBC’s The Celebrity Apprentice and as guest-starring roles on series which include The Middle, Entourage, Veronica Mars, 8 Simple Rules, and Community. Lisa made her Broadway debut in June 2007 in Chicago as Roxie Hart.

From 2002-2006, Lisa hosted Soapnet’s talk show Soap Talk and earned 4 Emmy nominations under the Outstanding Talk Show Host category. She has also written 3 books: The Big, Fun, Sexy Sex Book, Starlit, and Rinnavation (The New York Times best-seller). Further, her other ventures include a fashion line, ‘The Lisa Rinna Collection’ for QVC, and the cosmetics collection named Rinna Beauty.

Lisa Rinna Officially Confirmed Exit From Real Housewife

The longtime star of “Real Housewife,” Lisa Rinna, has officially confirmed her exit from the show; she joined the cast in 2014. In a statement made to People, the reality star thanked everyone at Bravo and all people involved in the series. She added that it was fun working. The outlet on Thursday published this statement on. 

At the end of last season, Lisa’s contract reportedly expired, and it was mutually decided with the network that she would not return. 

Since season 5, Lisa has been part of the show; she is one of the most iconic cast members, along with some other notable co-stars, which include Erika Jayne, Kyle Richards, Kathy Hilton, and many more. 

In the show’s recent season, the 12th season, Lisa lost her mother, Lois Rinna, quickly after production began, which made Lisa difficult.  

But her show exit isn’t a big surprise to several fans, as Lisa has given hints about her possible departure for some time. 

Lisa Rinna Early Life:

Lisa was born on 11th July 1963 in Newport Beach, California. She is of Portuguese, Italian, and English descent. When she was 7, her family relocated to Medford, Oregon, where she spent her childhood. After graduating, Lisa moved to San Francisco, California, to pursue her modeling career and started her career steps by appearing in commercials. 

Lisa Rinna Family Background:

Lisa’s parents are Lois Irene (née DeAndrade) and Frank Rinna. She had an older half-sister named Laurene, who passed away when Lisa was around seven. 

On 15th November 2021, Lisa announced that her mother had died after a stroke.

Lisa Rinna Education:

Lisa attended Hoover Elementary in Medford, and in 1981, she graduated from Medford Senior High School.

Lisa Rinna Physical stats:

Lisa stands 5 ft 7 in tall and weighs around 58 kg. Further, she has dark brown hair and pretty hazel-colored eyes. Her distinctive features include a short-cropped hairdo and her stylish personality. 

Lisa has acknowledged having plastic surgery and Botox, silicone, and Juvéderm injections.

Lisa Rinna Birth Signs:

According to Lisa’s birthdate, she comes under the Cancer zodiac. Currently, she is 59 years old. Her birth flower is Water Lily, and her birth element is Water. 

Lisa Rinna Net worth:

Lisa estimated net worth is around $10 million. Her networth includes earnings from acting, modeling, endorsements, business, etc.

Lisa Rinna Relationship Status: 

Rinna married actor Harry Hamlin on 29th March 1997 in Beverly Hills, California. They have two daughters, Delilah Belle (born on 10th June 1998) and Amelia Gray (born on 13th June 2001). Lisa also has a stepson, Dimitri Alexander, the son of Harry and Ursula Andress.

Lisa Rinna Career:

Acting:

At age 21, Lisa appeared in the music video for the single “Naughty Naughty” for John Parr. Later, in 1990, she appeared as the girlfriend of Jason Bateman’s character in many episodes of The Hogan Family. After two years, Lisa received national recognition when she appeared on NBC’s Days of Our Lives as Billie Reed. In 1995, she departed though she reprised the role in 2002, 2012, and 2018. Further, from 1996 to 1998, Lisa switched from daytime to primetime and was portrayed as Taylor McBride on Aaron Spelling’s Melrose Place. She has starred in 2 projects opposite Harry Hamlin, in the teen drama Veronica Mars and the Lifetime television film Sex, Lies & Obsession. In 1999, she also appeared in the film, Another Woman’s Husband, which was the highest-rated movie. In 2001, Lisa was featured as Veronica Simpson in the comedy film Good Advice alongside Denise Richards and Charlie Sheen.

From June to July 2007, Lisa starred in the Broadway production, in the role of Roxie Hart, alongside Harry, who played the character Billy Flynn. That same year, on HBO’s Entourage, she portrayed Donna Devaney in the episode “Malibooty”‘.The following year, she appeared in Disney Channel’s musical television series Hannah Montana as Mr. Dontzig’s cousin Francesca. Lisa also guest-starred on an episode of Movie Stars. In 2011, she guest-starred as Brooke Diamond on Nickelodeon’s Big Time Rush. Further, she had guest roles in several series, like C.S.I., The Guest Book, The Hotwives of Las Vegas, Awkward, and This Close.

Hosting and reality television:

From 2002 to 2006, Lisa hosted Soap Talk, which aired on Soapnet. Further, in 2006, she competed in the 2nd season of Dancing with the Stars along with professional dancer Louis van Amstel. In August 2007, she became the host of TV Guide Network’s red carpet coverage after replacing Joan Rivers. In addition, Lisa subsequently guest-hosted Live with Regis and Kelly and the TV makeover program Merge on Lifetime.

In 2008, TV Guide reported that Lisa and her husband Harry had signed a deal to create a reality television series based on their family life. This Harry Loves Lisa series premiered on 6th October 2010 on TV Land. In 2010, Lisa was featured as a guest judge on the 4th episode of the 2nd season of RuPaul’s Drag Race. The following year, she participated in 4th season of The Celebrity Apprentice. Further, she returned to the show in its 6th season. On 13th May 2013, Lisa appeared in the Late Night episode with Jimmy Fallon and Lil Jon. In 2014, she participated in ABC’s game show Sing Your Face Off.

In 2014, Lisa joined the cast of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills in its 5th season. On 6th January 2023, she announced about leaving the show after 8 seasons.

Modeling:

In 1998, when Lisa was six months pregnant with her 1st daughter posed for a nude pictorial and cover shoot for Playboy magazine’s September issue. In May 2009, she was again a cover model for Playboy. 

Lisa has also been on the covers of Living Well, CVLUX, Soap Opera Magazine, and Soap Opera Digest. Throughout her career, she appeared in several commercials and print ads. In 2008, Lisa appeared in print ads for Mars Inc’s M&M candies and Joey Fatone for their advertising campaign. Further, in 2012, she appeared in the abdominal muscle toner “The Flex Belt” alongside Denise Richards, Adrianne Curry, and Janet Evans. Lisa has also appeared in a TV commercial for Taco Bell and Winsor Pilates. She modeled that same year to benefit the charity Dress for Success.

Additionally, for Depend, she appeared in print ads and commercials. In 2019, she walked the runway for Kyle Richards’ and Shahida Clayton’s clothing brand at New York Fashion Week. 

In 2008, Lisa released a series of workout videos titled Dance Body Beautiful.

Fashion:

Lisa and Harry jointly owned the Belle Gray clothing boutique in Sherman Oaks, California; they started it in 2011 and, in 2012, closed the store because of their busy schedules in acting careers. In June 2019, Lisa launched an activewear collaboration along with retailer Goldsheep. Further, this collaboration benefited The Trevor Project.

QVC:

In April 2012, Lisa brought her collection to QVC, titled the Lisa Rinna Collection, which was successfully ruined.

Cosmetics:

In September 2020, Lisa announced that she would launch Rinna Beauty with lip kits named No Apologies, Birthday Suit, and Legends Only.

Beverages:

In August 2022, Lisa announced on one of the episodes of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills that she is entering the alcoholic beverage industry and has named the product Rinna Rosé.

Lisa Rinna Social Appearance:

Lisa has an active profile on Instagram and Facebook with around 3.5M and 750K followers, respectively. She has a vast collection of pictures from her professional and personal life.
