Bella Hadid Just Blessed Our Feeds With Several Bikini Photos

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, September 16, 2024

Bella Hadid is continuing to soak up that sun with not one, not two, but several bikini snaps, proving that hot girl summer is a year-round state of mind. On Sunday, the model shared some bikini-clad photos to both her Instagram Story and main feed.

In a gallery post, Bella modeled a brown velvet bikini with gold ring accents. She paired the bikini top with unexpected green and white short-shorts, as well as stacks of beaded bracelets on either wrist. Hadid wore her long brown tresses in disheveled beach-y waves that she whipped around in the photos.

Over on her story, Hadid modeled more suits, including a black two-piece that she paired with a sun hat while lounging in a pool, as well as a bright yellow bikini that she wore while arching her back over the edge.

Earlier in the weekend, the model shared some BTS footage from her James Bond-inspired Michael Kors photoshoot, where she wore an ultra-cropped Agent 007 tee with black bikini bottoms. Just putting it out there, but we'd love to see Bella as the next Bond girl.
