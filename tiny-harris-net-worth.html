Tiny Harris Net Worth: An Analysis of American Singers Wealth

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, August 2, 2024

American singer and reality show star Tameka Cottle is better known by her stage name, Tiny Harris. Cottle became well-known once she joined Xscape, a successful R&B group.

Tameka is credited with writing several popular songs, including “No Scrubs” by TLC and “Shape of You,” one of Ed Sheeran’s most successful singles. Cottle is a phenomenal composer and performer, but she has also dabbled in reality TV with appearances on shows like “Tiny and Toya” and “T.I. & Tiny: The Family Hustle” which star Tameka’s husband, the rapper T.I., and their children.

How Much is Tiny Harris’s Net Worth?

According to sources, Tiny Harris’s net worth is $ 5 million. Tameka was a member of the R&B group Xscape while a student at Tri-Cities Performing Arts High School in 1992. At his birthday party in Atlanta, Georgia, Jamie Dupri heard Xscape perform and promptly signed them to his record label, “So So Def Recordings.”

Cottle was a member of the band for a long time and during that time they released three platinum albums: “Hummin Cummin at ‘Cha”, “Off the Hook” and “Traces of My Lipstick.” Two “Soul Train Music Awards” were given to her and the rest of Xscape for “Best New R&B Album.”

Tameka Cottle Harris Journey From Reality TV to Entrepreneurship

Tameka Cottle Harris, now 47 years old received a Grammy in 2000 for best R&B song with Kandi for their work on TLC’s smash hit, No Scrubs. At least eight reality shows have featured Tiny since 2009. Meanwhile, the singer tried her hand at entrepreneurship on 2014’s Tiny And Shekinah’s Weave Tour, recording while on the road with a mobile hair salon.

Tameka has many other accomplishments such as being a member of the “American Society of Composers,” receiving a “Authors and Publishers” award in the categories R&B and Pop Music for “No Scrubs” along with the “ASCAP Rap” award for her work on the track “No Pigeons” by Sporty Thievz.

Cottle has shared the stage with the likes of Bow Wow, T.I., Lil’ Kim and 8Ball & MJG among many others. Xscape’s Tameka Scott returned after a five-year hiatus to record the album “Unchained” with her sisters LaToucha and Tameka Scott and new member Kiesha Miles. In 2005, Xscape issued the single “What’s Up.”

Cottle has collaborated with Kiesha Miles on a project and the resulting musical trio goes by the name “OMG Girlz.” Zonnique, Tameka’s daughter is also a member of the group. T.I.’s “Hello” music video featured Cottle, her family and the OMG Girlz.

Tameka Cottle is well-known as a TV personality as well as a musician. Tina and Toya starring Cottle and Antonia “Tonya” Wright, Lil’ Wayne’s ex-wife aired on VH1. In June 2009, the series premiered.

On July 22, 2014, Cottle released her first solo single, “What the F__k You Gon Do?” under her new label name, “Pretty Hustle.” In less than 24 hours after its release, the song climbed to the top of the R&B/soul charts on iTunes, peaking at number five.
