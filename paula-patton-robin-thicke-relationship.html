An Inside Look At Paula Patton And Ex-Husband Robin Thicke's Relationship

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, July 13, 2024

Robin Thicke and Paula Patton were once a fan favorite Hollywood "it" couple. They were teenage sweethearts who later married but called it quits after nine years of wedded bliss. Their divorce left many people wondering, what happened between them? Things got pretty bad between the two, accusations were made, and a bitter custody battle of their son Julian ensued.

The famous exes are in a better place now. Robin and Paula attended co-parenting therapy and are co-parenting amicably these days. However, the drama surrounding their split lives on.

Robin's no stranger to controversy, the singer has made several headlines in the past, for all the wrong reasons. The performer was even 'Canceled' after Emily Ratajkowski claimed he groped her on the set of 'Blurred Lines.' The music video, however, was seemingly just the beginning of the singer's troubles.

Their High Profile Divorce Made Headlines

As far as celebrity divorces go, Robin and Paula's was among some of the most publicized at the time. He was at the height of his career, was involved in some controversies, and people were wondering whether he'd be canceled for good. His success had fueled some destructive behaviors, which undoubtedly contributed to the ending of his marriage.

Paula and Robin met in high school and dated through college. They were married for nine years when Paula filed for divorce in 2014. What followed was a he-said-she-said battle, and there were cheating accusations and even allegations of abuse.

Following the child abuse allegations made against Thicke, the singer was investigated by the L.A. County Department of Child and Family Services. Per US Weekly, it was alleged that he'd spanked his and Paula's son, Julian.

Robin was even issued a restraining order after Paula leveled physical abuse allegations against him. The actor also accused Thicke of infidelity, but he denied both allegations.

They Went To Co-Parenting Therapy

Their contentious split resulted in a bitter custody battle of their son, Julian. Owing to the allegations against the 'Blurred Lines' singer, Patton was awarded temporary sole custody of their son. However, per reports, the pair eventually reached an agreement over Julian's custody.

Despite the shared custody of Julian, Robin and Paula's relationship had broken down, and they needed to find a way to co-parent better. They went to co-parenting therapy, which helped them communicate better. Co-parenting therapy helped mend their tumultuous relationship, thus being better parents to Julian.

In an interview with Extra TV, Thicke revealed, "Over the last seven years, I’ve been through just about everything a human being can go through… I lost my father. My house burned down. I’ve been divorced. I fell in love…"

He continued, "Well, when we were first going through divorce, there was bickering… and then we used co-parenting couples therapy and that really opened the door for communication in a much better way for us. And our son is thriving, our son Julian…. And nothing makes you prouder as parents when you had some tough times… figured it out, and your son is thriving because of it."

They're In A Good Place Now

Patton and Thicke's relationship has without a doubt improved tremendously. The exes have both moved on with their lives. Robin is engaged to April Love Geary, and the couple has three children together. It's unclear whether Paula is seeing anyone, as she hasn't publicly been linked to anyone romantically in years.

With their issues a thing of the past, it seems that everything worked out for them. They have repaired their relationship and are in a good place now. Both have had nothing but positive things to say about each other in interviews.

Despite, their failed marriage, it's clear to see that the celebs have a deep respect for one another. In an interview with PEOPLE, Robin dished on his relationship with Paula now.

The crooner said, "There's so much emotion when you first break up, and there's so much stuff that spills over. But time heals those wounds, and she's an incredible person. Once you can put yourself second, your son rises to the top, and you go, 'It's not about us anymore.' We're very lucky the way everything has worked out."

He added, "My marriage to Paula was crumbling. I started using painkillers. It was a melting pot of trouble brewing, and I was so arrogant that I thought I could handle it all."

Working on their relationship seems to have yielded positive results for Julian. Robin shares pictures of his cute family often, in which all four of his kids are ever-present.

"We're co-parenting at our very best, and it shows in Julian's results, Thicke reveals. "He's thriving and has really blossomed this last year. He's so devoted to school. He's the opposite of me: I was the class clown in the back row. He's in the front of class and loves going to school."
