Dominick Cruz is a Mexican mixed martial artist from San Diego. To his credit, he has won the UFC Bantamweight Championship many times and has achieved second place in the official UFC Bantamweight Rankings. With a blue belt in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, he also has an impressive record in the UFC/WEC bantamweight category. He has also appeared as an MMA analyst for Fox Sports. His orthodox stance of fighting and winning series has garnered him a huge following, with followers reaching over 400,000 on Facebook, over 500,000 on Twitter, and over 900,000 on Instagram.

    Dominick Cruz Facts

  • As a child, he lived with his mother, grandmother, and brother in a trailer park in Tucson.
  • He considers his mother to be his greatest hero.
  • For him, fighting in the UFC means being among the best fighters in the world.
  • His first job was as a customer service representative for Lowe’s home improvement business.
  • Before Cruz started fighting full-time, he planned to be a firefighter, so he entered community college.
  • He has also contributed as an MMA analyst for Fox Sports .
  • His least favorite food, which he still somehow eats, is kale.
  • His nickname “The Dominator” began as a joke after his high school football teammates named him for his strength playing defensively at the linebacker position.
  • His only pro loss was against Urijah Faber during the WEC Featherweight title fight on March 24, 2007.
  • Cruz was sidelined from October 2011 to September 2014 due to his extended injury and recovery time.
  • He has a dog named Dogo.
  • He supports the Veteran Village Foundation .
  • In 2010 he was named Fighter of the Year by USA Today .
