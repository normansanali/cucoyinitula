Who is Avi Akbar?

Avi Akbar is an American TikToker and social media star. He is famously known for his POV and comedy videos, which he uploads on his TikTok account.

How old is Avi Akbar?

The TikTok sensation was born on December 25, 2000, in California, the United States. As of 2023, Avi is 21 years old holding an American nationality.

Similarly, his ethnicity is Caucasian while his birth sign is Capricorn.

He was brought up in a well-settled Christian family. However, he is yet to share the details about his parents, siblings, and early educational life.
