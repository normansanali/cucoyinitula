Alodia Gosiengfiao Wikipedia

Alodia Gosiengfiao was born on March 9, 1988, in Quezon City, Philippines. She also goes by her other names, Edj and Queen Siopao. 

Alodia became a cosplayer after she was introduced to an anime and video game enthusiasts forum called Anime Club. She was encouraged by her friends at the forum to do cosplay.

She started her cosplay journey in 2003 when she was just 15 years old and joined various competitions in Metro Manila. Since then, she has been a cosplay enthusiast and part of countless events and competitions.

She was also invited to various international cosplay events because of her fame in the Asian cosplay communities. 

Alodia started her Twitch career in 2014. She has constantly been streaming her gaming video ever since. She plays Dota 2 and League of Legends. 

She recently married Christopher Quimbo in February and is enjoying her married life.
