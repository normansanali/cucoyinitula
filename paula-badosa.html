Paula Badosa- Professional Career

Paula began playing tennis at the ‘Playa de Aro’ club after relocating to Barcelona. She moved to Valencia and then back to Barcelona when she was 17, in order to advance her tennis career. In September 2012, she made her sporting debut on the ITF Junior Circuit. In February 2014, she won her first junior singles championship at the Grade-1 Mediterranee Avenir in Casablanca, and in April of the same year, she won her first junior doubles title at the Trofeo Juan Carlos Ferrero in Villena. She made her Grand Slam debut in 2014, reaching the quarterfinals in both singles and doubles.

More success came her way when she was runner-up in singles at the European Junior Championships. Her junior career came to a conclusion at the 2015 French Open, where she won the Girls’ singles title. Later in 2012, she began playing on the ITF Women’s Circuit in Getxo, where in November 2013, she won her maiden title in Saint Jordi, and in March 2015, she obtained a wildcard for the main tournament at the ‘Premier Mandatory Miami Open’ and secured her first two WTA Tour victories.
