Paul Rudd Wiki/Biography

Born on 06 April 1969, Paul Rudd’s age is 52 years as of April 2021. He was born and brought up in a middle-class Christian family from Passaic, New Jersey, the United States. He is an American by nationality and believes in the Jewish religion.

He completed his early schooling at the Broadmoor Junior High School, the US, and later enrolled himself at the Shawnee Mission West High School.

After that, he enrolled himself at the University of Kansas, the American Academy of Dramatic Arts, and the British American Drama Academy from where he completed his further studies. Since childhood, he has always wanted to pursue his career in acting. He also used to do a lot of theatre during his high school and college days.
