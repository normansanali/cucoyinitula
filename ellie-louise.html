Ellie Louise is a young British YouTuber who posts content on gymnastics, beauty, fashion, and fitness on her self-titled personal YouTube channel. Ellie Louise is also a part of the collaborative channel ‘Seven Perfect Angels,’ where she holds the Friday slot.

Early Life

Ellie Louise came to this world on September 20, 2001. She was born and raised in Hertfordshire, England. At present, she is 21 years old and her birth sign is Virgo. Talking about her family information, she has a sister named Sophie, and four step-siblings, including a sister named Erin. Her mother’s name is Carla. There is no information about her educational background. However, judging by her age, she might be a high school graduate.

Career

Ellie was active, confident, and artistic from a young age. To help her develop into a well-rounded personality, her parents arranged for her to get training in gymnastics when she was four years old. Ellie proved to be naturally skilled at the sport and it did not take her long to bloom into a graceful gymnast who could perform perfectly timed moves.

She was previously a part of the collab channel Seven Funtastic Girls beginning in October of 2014. Moreover, she is also known as Ellie the Unicorn. Ellie was just 12 when she auditioned for the SAKs channels and easily cleared it. Her gymnastic skills helped her in acing the audition!

Soon, she found herself a part of the YouTube channel ‘Seven Funtastic Girls’ where she was featured on several popular videos like ‘The day Ellie became a Gymnast!’ and ‘Ellie’s Missing Mermaid Powers!’ With time, the preteen gained more visibility and charmed the audience with her gymnastic skills, witty one-liners, and of course, her cute and innocent looks. Likewise, the most viewed video on the channel is “Back to School Morning Routine & What’s in my Backpack!“.

More…

Her increasing popularity led her to become a part of various well-known SAKs channels, such as ‘Seven Super Girls,’ ‘Seven Twinkling Tweens,’ and ‘Seven Perfect Angels.’ Along with appearing on these collab channels, Ellie started her self-titled channel where she began posting videos on gymnastics, beauty, fashion, and fitness.

Even though her channel is not as popular as the collab ones, it is surely growing in prominence with each day. Some of her most popular videos are ‘Gymnastics Truth or Dare!,’ ‘How to do the Splits,’ and ‘How Ellie Became a Living Doll!’.
