Ade Super Hotel Ondo, Ondo Town

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, August 16, 2024

Ade Super Hotel is a budget 2-Star hotel that provides guests with the perfect opportunity to work and relax. It has become one of the relaxation destination of dignitaries, statesmen, foreign potentates and celebrities visiting Ondo town.

Ade Super Hotel is equipped and complemented with modern amenities to ensure and guarantee the satisfaction of its guest. These rooms are tendered at different prices and are grouped into: Single room, Super Double, New Double Super and Kings Room. Amenities in these rooms include flat-screen televisions, Wi-Fi access, Key Card system, Air conditioner, 24 hours electricity, wardrobes, reading tables, chairs and private bathrooms.

It provides an array of tastefully prepared African and Continental dishes at the on-site restaurant. The guests can also order premium brands of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks from the on-site bar. It also has a fitness centre that is equipped to help guests burn some fat and get in shape.

Other services at Ade Super Hotel include: a business centre, laundry service, car hire service, Suya spot, POS, Event Hall, Taxi pickup, adequate parking space and 24-hour security.

Terms and Conditions
Check In: From 12:00 pm (ID Required)
Check Out: By 12:00 pm
Pets: Pets are not allowed

FAQs

A: Yes, It offers free Wi-Fi for its guests.

A: No. It does not have a swimming pool.

Q: Does Ade Super Hotel offer complimentary breakfast?
A: No, Ade Super Hotel does not offer complimentary breakfast.

Q: Is there a fitness centre/gym at Ade Super Hotel?
A: Yes, there is a fitness centre at the hotel.

A: Yes, Ade Super Hotel has an on-site restaurant.
