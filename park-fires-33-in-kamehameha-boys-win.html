Park fires 33 in Kamehameha boys win | News, Sports, Jobs

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, July 23, 2024

Lucas Park shot a 33 as the Kamehameha Schools Maui boys golf team defeated King Kekaulike 151-241 at Pukalani Country Club on Wednesday.

Jacob Puu had a 37, Cade Harris posted a 39 and Kainalu Steward scored a 42 for the Warriors (3-0).

Austin Ferreira shot a 52, Thomas Peterson and Ian Manibog each had a 62 and Jordan Beesly recorded a 65 for Na Alii (0-3).

At Kapalua Bay Course, Rhett Wiseman and Lance Ito each shot a 39 as the Lunas (3-0) remained unbeaten.

Kaimi Kanaha had a 40 and Kerek Franz recorded a 41 for the Lunas.

Adam Quandt shot a 39, Nick Head had a 67, Zac Giles recorded a 69 and Coleman Kingwell shot a 78 for Na Pueo (2-1).
