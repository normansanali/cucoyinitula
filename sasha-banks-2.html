Career

Sasha Banks started her career on the independent circuit. When she moved to Woburn, Massachusetts in 2008, she started training at Chaotic Wrestling. She made her professional wrestling debut on 1 October 2010, in an intergender tag team bout with Nikki Roxx against Alexxis and Danny E. in a losing effort.

She and Roxx rapidly allied, partnering together frequently and escorting each other to ringside. Danny E defeated her in her singles debut on 22 October and again on 13 November in a rematch.

For her first win, she teamed up with Roxx to beat Alexis and Mistress Belmont in a tag team match on 7 January 2011. On 11 February, she attempted to become the first Chaotic Wrestling Women’s Champion by competing in a five-woman gauntlet match.

She competed in singles contests throughout the rest of 2011 and beat Alexxis in an “I Quit” match on 2 December to win the Chaotic Wrestling Women’s Championship.

6 January 2012 was the day when she successfully defended her championship against Barbie. She met Luscious Latasha with the championship on the line on 27 January, where she retained the crown. In a rematch, KV defeated Alexxis to retain his title.

She subsequently successfully defended her title against Nikki Roxx on two separate occasions. In a final four-way match on 1 June, she beat Alexxis, Barbie, and Mistress Belmont to retain the title, and she went on to shatter Alexxis’ previous record of 182 days as the longest-reigning Chaotic Wrestling Women’s Champion.
