FKA Twigs Puts a Western Spin on an Ultra-Feminine Outfit

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, July 6, 2024

Cowboy boots are still having a moment.

While stepping out for dinner in West Hollywood, FKA Twigs eschewed heels for the Western-themed footwear to style with her flowing midi dress. The dress, which the "cellophane" singer layered over a white halter-neck top, featured a colorful abstract print and a thigh-high leg slit, which perfectly showed off the rustic, caramel-colored boots. She kept with the cowboy theme in a cropped tan suede jacket and a turquoise oval concho belt wrapped around her waist. Finally, she accessorized with a dark gray nylon handbag.

Twigs has had a busy year thus far with the release of her debut mixtape, Caprisongs, which features musical cameos from artists like The Weeknd, Jorja Smith, Rema, and Shygirl. She has since dropped the accompanying music videos for "tears in the club," "papi bones," "ride the dragon," "oh my love," and more.

The singer opened up about the project in a January interview with Variety.

"My other projects really touched on personal, very specific things that I’ve gone through in my life. That’s usually how I write," she said. "In the pandemic, though, there was a shared adversity, a shared sadness and loss. Because we all lost something on some scale—be it our freedom, our routine, love, a sense of security. I think it was the first time as an artist that I felt the desire to create something for other people. It would be naïve to believe that it made them feel better … but I did want to provide truth, honesty, light and joy to people, to remind them what we had, and are grateful for. Everybody needed support during this time. I certainly had with my friends and family, and I wanted to share that with the world."
